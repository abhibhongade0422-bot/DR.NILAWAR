Our Story

The Nilawar Smile Care Legacy

Built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and genuine patient care.

Nilawar Smile Care was founded with a single mission: to provide the people of Nagpur with dental care that rivals the best clinics in the country. Located at 05, Sodaday Complex, 1st Floor, WHC Road, Gokulpeth, our clinic has grown from a small practice into one of the city's most recognized dental centers.

Every aspect of the clinic is designed for patient comfort. From the moment you walk in, our team ensures you feel welcome. We use state-of-the-art digital diagnostics, advanced sterilization protocols, and the latest cosmetic techniques to deliver outstanding results — painlessly.

Whether you need a routine cleaning, a complex implant procedure, or a complete smile makeover, Dr. Nilawar and his team are here for you. Our patients aren't just numbers — they're family.
